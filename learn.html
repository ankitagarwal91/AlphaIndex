HedgeACT Alpha Index

The HedgeACT Alpha Index represents an equally weighted compilation of alpha generating strategies contained in the HedgeACT database. These strategies have been selected for the index based on the research HedgeACT has conducted to determine the optimal risk adjusted alpha generating strategies that are not correlated to the broader asset classes. Data for the index calculations are derived directly from the data tables contained within the HedgeACT database. The HedgeACT Alpha Index is not an investable product and no information about any investable products can be provided unless certain statutory and regulatory conditions are met. To learn more about the strategies contained in the HedgeACT Alpha Index, including investable products and associated important information, admonishments, and limitations, you must be an accredited investor and qualified client and register with HedgeACT.
